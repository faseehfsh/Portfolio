ANIMATED SHORT FILM

Embark on a heartwarming animated journey with Rohit, a young university student filled with the excitement and challenges that come with starting a new chapter. Join Rohit as he navigates the world of virtual classes, facing the struggle of attention and shyness in a sea of 400 students.

In this captivating story, Rohit's transition from online to physical classes becomes a pivotal moment of self-discovery. As he makes new friends, conquers his fears, and learns to embrace vulnerability, the narrative unfolds into a tale of resilience and personal growth.

Witness Rohit's transformative experience as he overcomes his shyness through an ingenious solution and finds the courage to share his thoughts and experiences. The story beautifully captures the essence of facing challenges, seeking support, and the empowering impact of self-expression.

This heartening animated video not only resonates with students but serves as an inspirational reminder that everyone has their unique journey to confidence. Share in Rohit's triumphs, and be inspired by the universal themes of resilience and self-discovery that unfold throughout his university adventure.

Join us on this animated odyssey that seamlessly blends the digital and real world, reminding us all that growth often comes from embracing our fears and finding our voice in unexpected places.
